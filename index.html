KADEX-2016

Tactic Pro Ltd. took active participation in the IV KADEX International Exhibition on ammunition and military technical property, which was hold in Astana, Kazakhstan between June 2 to 5, 2016.

Exposition of Tactic Pro Ltd. presented advanced equipment and technology of the world leading companies from Israel, Europe, USA in the sphere of development and production of ballistic protection, electro-optics, detection, tracking and control equipment, active acoustic emitters and communication system devices, advanced military equipment and accessories, personal protection, multispectral camouflage systems, high-speed armored vehicles, tactical quad-vehicles, water purifying systems, special devices and many other things.

As a participant in the exhibition Tactic Pro Ltd. was awarded with Diploma by the Minister of Defense of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Mr. Imangali Nurgaliyevich Tasmagambetov and Kadex-2016 Gold Medal of Honor.

Tactic Pro Ltd. appreciates our Partners and Customers for participation and great attention to the exposition of Tactic Pro Ltd.!

KADEX-2012

Tactic Pro Ltd. took active part in the “KADEX-2012” International Exhibition of Weapons Systems and Military Equipment.

Tactic Pro Ltd. presented the world leading companies from Israel and USA having activities in the sphere of development and production of electro-optical devices, equipment for through-wall monitoring and detection of objects, active devices for communication systems, long-range acoustic device, tactical and command systems, advanced tactical equipment and accessories to improve the efficiency of small arms usage, small-sized unmanned aerial vehicle, advanced ballistic systems for ground and air vehicles protection, tactical light-armored high-speed vehicles, as well as state-of-the-art developments in the sphere of load-carriage systems and personal protection systems in the “KADEX-2012”.

Minister of Defense of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Adilbek Ryskeldinovich Dzhaksybekov, awarded the Tactic Pro Ltd. with Diploma and “KADEX-2012” Gold Medal for the active participation in the Exhibition.
